# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of **Koharu-TH** pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment for our community include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting or harassing language, or personal attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Discriminatory jokes and language
- Inappropriate or unwelcome aggression toward any person
- Posting or sharing private or personal information without explicit consent

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by emailing **conduct@koharu-th.dev**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers are obligated to:

- Take any report seriously
- Act impartially and in good faith
- Preserve the confidentiality of the reporter and any parties involved, to the extent possible

Violations may result in:

- A warning
- Temporary or permanent ban from the project’s communication channels
- Removal of contributed content

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces related to the **Koharu-TH** project, including but not limited to the project's GitHub repository, issue trackers, pull‑request discussions, mailing lists, Discord/Slack channels, and any other online or offline events organized by the community.

## Contact

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have concerns about how a situation was handled, please contact the project’s Code of Conduct team at **conduct@koharu-th.dev**. If you feel unsafe contacting us directly, you may also reach out to a trusted community member for mediation.
